Final Exam Info

The CS106A Final Exam is Monday March 19th from 8:30AM to 11:30AM PST.

Download Your Final Exam

Download your encrypted final: The encrypted finals are ready to download! You should download the exam in advance of arriving at your exam location. Warning: Make sure to download the correct exam -- otherwise the password you are given in your exam room won't match (sad times).

Instructions:

All students should download and test their exam program before the final!

On a Windows PC, download the .zip file and completely extract it into another folder. Then open the BlueBook program by double-clicking. If you get a Windows message that says, "Windows protected your PC," click on "More info" and then "Run anyway".

On a mac, download the .zip file and completely extract it. Find the BlueBook app inside the folder and right or control-click the app. Chose the menu option 'open', which should be the first choice. If prompted, click on Open again to confirm you want to run the program.
